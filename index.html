Our Story

A beloved corner of sweetness at the heart of Shalimar Garden

Where Tradition Meets Taste

Nestled at the bustling Shiv Chowk in Shalimar Garden, Sahibabad, Bikaner Sweet & Bakers has been a cherished destination for those who crave the authentic flavors of Indian mithai and street food.

From the crispy golden Jalebi dripping with syrup to the perfectly spiced Samosas and hearty Chole Bhature — every item is prepared fresh daily with time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick chai and snack, picking up a box of sweets for a celebration, or sitting down for a full plate of Pav Bhaji — you'll find the warmth of home cooking in every bite.
